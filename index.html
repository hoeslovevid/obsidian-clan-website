// About the Legion

We are Tenno united under one oath: to stand together in the Origin System, support each other in battle, and build a community that lasts.

◆

Our Mission

The Obsidian Oath Legion is a community of Tenno bound by loyalty and excellence. We run missions together, build dojos, and support each other across the Origin System.

◆

What We Offer

Active squads, clan research, dojo access, and a friendly Discord where we coordinate, share builds, and hang out. All platforms welcome.

◆

The Oath

We stand by our word. Respect, teamwork, and dedication define us. When you join the Legion, you join a family—not just a clan.

◆

Platforms

We welcome Tenno on PC, PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch. Cross-play has made it easier than ever to squad up—our Discord brings everyone together.

◆

Community & Events

From weekly runs and build shares to seasonal events and giveaways, we keep the Legion active. Join Discord to stay in the loop and find squads.

◆

Dojo & Research

Our dojo is built and maintained by the Legion. Contribute when you can—every resource and forma helps unlock research and rooms for everyone.

// Legion Rules

01

Respect Everyone

No harassment, toxicity, or discrimination. Treat fellow Tenno and clan members with respect in-game and on Discord.

02

Stay Active

We expect reasonable activity. Inactive members may be removed to make room for new recruits—rejoin anytime if life gets busy.

03

No Scamming or Exploits

Fair play only. No scamming in trades, no exploit abuse. Violations result in removal.

04

Represent the Legion

When you wear the clan tag, you represent us. Keep behavior in public missions and Region chat in line with our values.

05

Contribute When You Can

Help with dojo research, events, or resources when possible. Every contribution strengthens the Legion—no pressure, but participation is appreciated.

06

No Begging or Spam

Don’t spam chat or repeatedly ask for carries, handouts, or trades. Ask politely once; respect the answer.

07

Follow Discord Rules

Our Discord has its own rules and channels. Read them and follow them—they keep the community organized and friendly.

08

Help Newer Tenno

Be willing to answer questions and help newer players when you can. We grow stronger when we lift each other up.

// How to Join

1

Join Our Discord

Head to our Discord server (link below). Introduce yourself and let us know you want to join the clan.

2

Leave Your Current Clan

If you’re in another clan, leave it from the Clan menu in-game so we can send you an invite.

3

Receive Your Invite

An officer will send you a clan invite in Warframe. Accept it and you’re in the Legion.

Have questions? Ask in Discord—we’re happy to help.
